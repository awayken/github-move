Villanelle Helper

The villanelle form of poetry has a fixed, refrained, almost meditative structure.

Villanelle poems have 19 lines consisting of five tercets (three-line stanzas) and ending with a quatrain (a five-line stanza). There are two repeating refrains that alternate and come together in the quatrain.

The villanelle is the structure of "Do not go gentle into that good night" by Dylan Thomas and "Mad Girl's Love Song" by Silvia Plath.
